Bye bye 2013, Welcome 2014

This is the last day of 2013. So it is the time for recapitulation.

What happend?

Well, first of all. I become a programmer. I was programming (with breaks) since I was a kid. But it was almost always just for the fun. I never took it seriously before.

But this year I've found Ruby and I felt in love with it.

I've started to learn a lot. Not just Ruby, but everything related to programming. I want to be a better programmer. I love Ruby, but I do not want to be just Ruby programmer. I want to be a Programmer.

I want to have my eyes open. To learn everything what is good and forget what is bad. I want to master it. I want to master tools which I use.

I've realized everything of this year. And next year I would like to take it to the next level.

So in 2014 I would like to join company where:

  • we will stricly use TDD
  • we will do code reviews
  • we will pair from time to time
  • we will have open eyes and we will embrace the change

Welcome 2014!
